STATISTICS

Philippines

Out of approximately 109 million people comprising the Philippine population, 12 million of them are part of the LGBTQ+ community. The 12 million people are approximately 11.01 percent of the population, including all genders.

World-Wide

The National Survey of Family Growth found results that were essentially the opposite of the UK survey with only 38% identifying as lesbian or gay compared to 62% identifying as bisexual. The National Survey of Sexual Health and Behavior and the Australian Longitudinal Study of Health and Relationships both found a majority of respondents (55% and 59%, respectively) identifying as bisexual. (UCLA School of Law, 2020)

Health Facts and Trivia
  • Over the last year, 52% of LGBTQ+ people have experienced depression.
  • 72% of bi women and 56% of bi men have experienced anxiety in the last year.
  • 13% of LGBTQ+ people have experienced some form of unequal treatment from the healthcare staff.
  • 20% of trans people have been pressured to access services to suppress their gender identity.
  • 14% of LGBT people have avoided treatment for fear of discrimination.
  • 46% of LGBT people feel able to open up about their sexual orientation to everyone in their family.
  • 11% of LGBTQ+ people have faced domestic abuse from their partner.
  • Almost 18% of LGBTQ+ people have experienced homelessnes at some point.
  • 35% of LGBTQ+ staff have hidden that they are LGBT at work for fear of discrimination.
  • 18% of of LGBTQ+ staff have been the target of negative comments or conduct from colleagues because they're LGBTQ+.
