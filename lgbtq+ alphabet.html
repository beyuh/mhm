LGBTQ+ ALPHABET

L- Lesbian T- Transgender Q- Questioning
Women who are attracted to people of the same sex. People whose gender identity is different from during birth. People who are exploring one's gender identity, expression, and sexual orientation.
G- Gay T- Transexual I- Intersex
Men who are attracted to people of their own sex. Sometimes used by women. People who have permenently changed their gender identity through surgery or hormones. People whose sexual anatomy and chromosomes don't fit under "male" and "female".
B- Bisexual Q- Queer A- Ally
People who are attracted to their own and opposite sex. Umbrella term that indicates the variations that make up the LGBTQ+ community. Non-queer people who support and advocate for the queer community.
A- Asexual P- Pansexual
People who don't feel any sexual desire or attraction to anyone. People who experience attraction to members of all gender identities.
